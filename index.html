Premier League

England · 2025/26 season

The Premier League is England's top football division, featuring 20 clubs competing from August to May. Renowned for its pace, physicality, and passionate crowds, it's consistently voted the world's most-watched domestic league. Founded in 1992, it attracts elite talent from every corner of the globe and produces some of football's most memorable moments week in, week out.

LaLiga

Spain · 2025/26 season

LaLiga is Spain's top professional football division, home to some of the greatest clubs and players the sport has ever seen. With a history stretching back to 1929, it has produced legendary rivalries — none bigger than El Clásico between Real Madrid and Barcelona. Known for its tactical sophistication and technical brilliance, LaLiga has claimed more UEFA Champions League titles than any other domestic league.

Bundesliga

Germany · 2025/26 season

Germany's Bundesliga is one of Europe's most exciting top flights, famous for its electric atmosphere, high-scoring matches, and some of the best attendances in world football. Founded in 1963, it has long been dominated by Bayern Munich, though clubs like Borussia Dortmund regularly challenge at the top. The Bundesliga is also celebrated for its commitment to fan culture and affordable ticket prices.

Serie A

Serie A is Italy's premier football division and one of the most storied leagues in the world. With passionate derbies and a rich tradition of producing world-class talent, Serie A remains a cornerstone of European football.

Ligue 1

France · 2025/26 season

Ligue 1 is the top tier of French football, featuring 18 clubs battling it out from August to May. While Paris Saint-Germain have dominated the modern era thanks to massive investment, the league is also a renowned breeding ground for world-class talent — France's pipeline of young stars is among the best on the planet. Clubs like Marseille, Lyon, and Monaco bring history, passion, and genuine European ambition to the table.

World Cup

International · 2026 edition
